About Us

We are Patriot Watch, a dedicated non-profit, non-governmental organization committed to upholding your digital rights, security, and privacy in the ever-changing digital landscape. Functioning as a vigilant watch-dog organisation, our role revolves around empowering individuals to navigate the intricacies of their online presence, all while advocating for a digital environment that's safer and more secure for everyone.


The Global Mission

This organisations founding mission is to enhance public awareness regarding the utmost significance of their digital presence and to furnish them with the knowledge and tools required for safeguarding their online rights, security, and privacy. Our unwavering dedication is directed towards nurturing a digital realm where individuals can flourish without the apprehension of cyber threats, intrusion, or undue surveillance.

Crucially, we uphold digital rights by rigorously scrutinizing and opposing unwarranted government surveillance intrusions, ensuring privacy is respected.


One Vision

We visualize a digital world where individuals can explore, learn, and connect without compromising their rights, security, and privacy. A world where corporations adhere to ethical standards, hackers channel their expertise into constructive contributions, and governments prioritize the well-being of their citizens' digital realm.
